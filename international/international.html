IAA Formation

The International Authentication Association is the industry body for suppliers and users of authentication technologies, systems and services. It aims are twofole: first, to educate and promote the use of authentication systems, technologies and products as an inegral part of effective stratigies to protect products, documents as an integral part of effective strategies to protect products, doctments and their users from couterfeiting and fraud; and second, to protect and promote the interests of the authentication industry (both users and suppliers) worldwide.

Authentifation technologies play a vital role in the protection strategies for products and documents worldwide. Yet the role and value of authentification in anto-couterfeiting is typically not examined nor well enough inderstood, largely because htere is no body promoting authentication.
